💾Save your project first

                
            

            

                

                    Your After Effects project hasn't been saved yet. Saving now lets the agent place all generated assets (images, audio, video, transcripts) in a clean, organized folder next to your project file — keeping everything portable and easy to back up.
                

                

                    
If you save, assets will be organized like this:

                    
YourProject.aep
└── aisist_assets/
    ├── images/
    ├── audio/    (TTS · music · SFX)
    ├── video/
    ├── svg/
    ├── transcripts/
    └── scripts/

                

                

                    
⚠ Continuing without saving means:

                    
    
                        
  • Generated assets land in your OS temp folder — they may be wiped on reboot or by system cleanup.
    • 
                        
  • Asset paths in your project will be absolute system paths, not relative — moving or sharing the project later will break links.
    • 
                        
  • You'll lose track of which assets belong to this composition once the temp folder is cleaned.
    • 
                        
  • No automatic backups of your work.
    •
